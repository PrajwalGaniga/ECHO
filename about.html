Introduction

ECHO is a vibrant, student-led club for CSD and ISE students that fosters collaboration, creativity, and technical growth. The club promotes sharing knowledge and innovation across computing, hacking, design, AI/ML, and culture. Our goal is to create an engaging environment where ideas resonate and expand widely, impacting both campus and beyond.

AI/ML Web Dev Cybersecurity Hackathons UI/UX Design Cultural Events

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a leading community empowering students through technology, creativity, and culture, where every idea finds room to grow and inspire.

Mission

  • Facilitate skill development through projects, hackathons, and workshops.
  • Encourage creative expression alongside technical expertise.
  • Build leadership, communication, and industry connections for holistic growth.

Organization Structure

Leadership

  • President (Core Lead) – The captain of the ship, represents the club everywhere🚢.
  • Vice President (Core Co-Lead) – The vice-captain ensuring smooth sailing ⛵.

Operations & Management

  • General Secretary – Documentation, scheduling, coordination 📑.
  • Joint Secretary – Assists secretary, keeps everyone on track 🧭.
  • Treasurer – Manages money, sponsorships, budgeting 💰.
  • Finance Co-Lead – Assists treasurer, keeps receipts safe 🧾.
  • Speaker – One who will be a active speaker for all the events.

Technical Divisions

  • Website & Tech Infrastructure – Our web wizards 🌐.
  • AI / ML / GenAI Division – Data, models, and the magic of AI 🤖.
  • Hackathon & Project Division – Coding marathons, project mentors 🏆.

Creative & Media

  • Design & Graphics Division – Posters, branding, creative magic ✨.
  • Social Media & Content Division – Insta reels 📸, YouTube edits 🎥, LinkedIn updates 💼.
  • Cultural & Event Division – Fun, cultural fests, and vibes 🎶.

Outreach & Growth

  • Workshops & Learning Division – Organizes technical sessions 📚.
  • PR & Outreach Division – Collabs, alumni, and industry connects 🤝.
